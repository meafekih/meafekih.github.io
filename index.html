About Me

Hi, my name is Amin and I work remotely as a freelance data analyst/scientist. I hold a Bachelor’s and Master’s degree in science (Business Analytics, VU University Amsterdam). Since I was young, I have always enjoyed to solve puzzles. So that’s how I look at big data sets: to me it is one big puzzle I want to solve. Finding patterns nobody else sees is the challenge to me. Before I started as a freelancer, I had worked for more than eight years as a data analyst in consulting and retail. I have worked for more than 25 companies which gives me the ability to translate complex questions into understandable insights. Do you want to work together? Please reach out to me by e-mail.
